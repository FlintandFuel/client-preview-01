Not the biggest coffee shop in Hilton. Just the best one, if you ask the regulars. Runners after their morning jog, remote workers who've claimed their corner table, parents straight after the school run. It already is.

Not a chain. Not a franchise. Just people who care about what comes out of the oven.

What We Do Here

A few things worth making the drive to Hilton for.

Specialty Espresso Bar

Flat whites, cappuccinos, Americanos, alternative brews and a Vietnamese iced coffee people come back for specifically. The full menu is in the section below.

Drink In · Take Away

Small-Batch Croissants & Pastries

Flaky, buttery, baked that morning. Almond croissants people turn their car around on Hilton Avenue for. Pain au chocolat, cheese croissants, custard Danish, cinnamon buns, and whatever else came out of the oven first. Arrive before they're gone.

Baked Fresh Daily

Artisan Sourdough Bread

True artisan sourdough. Country Bread, roasted onion and potato loaves, seasonal varieties. Baked here every morning. Take a loaf home and you'll be back for another.

Made On-Site · Worth the Trip

The Stormy Monday

Named after the blues standard, because some things just fit. Toasted egg and bacon. One regular called it the best in the world. We're not arguing. Order it before noon. Some mornings it doesn't make it that far.

House Favourite · Breakfast

Facebrick, Wood & a Wall of Guitars

Facebrick walls, solid timber, a wall of guitars that sets the tone before you've ordered a thing. Blues plays in the morning. Come in and feel it.

Real Atmosphere · No Pretence

Work-Friendly · Community-Driven

Free WiFi, indoor and veranda seating. Popular with remote workers, freelancers, and anyone who needs a good coffee and a bit of quiet. Stay as long as you need.
